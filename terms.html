Privacy Policy

隐私政策

Last Updated: October 2023

最后更新：2023年10月

YNM TechConnect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

壹纳米技术交流社区（"我们"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们在您使用我们的服务时如何收集、使用、披露和保护您的信息。

Information We Collect

我们收集的信息

We may collect the following types of information:

我们可能收集以下类型的信息：

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, Telegram username, and other contact details you provide when registering for our community.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and other communications you send to us.
  • Project Information: Details about projects you participate in, including collaboration history and performance metrics.
  • 个人信息：姓名、电子邮件地址、Telegram用户名以及您在注册我们的社区时提供的其他联系方式。
  • 技术信息：IP地址、浏览器类型、设备信息以及通过cookie和类似技术收集的使用数据。
  • 通信数据：您发送给我们的消息、反馈和其他通信。
  • 项目信息：关于您参与的项目的详细信息，包括协作历史和绩效指标。

How We Use Your Information

我们如何使用您的信息

We use the information we collect to:

我们使用收集的信息来：

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Facilitate project collaboration and communication
  • Send technical updates and community announcements
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our community
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • 提供、维护和改进我们的服务
  • 促进项目协作和沟通
  • 发送技术更新和社区公告
  • 回应您的询问并提供客户支持
  • 确保我们社区的安全和完整性
  • 遵守法律义务

Data Sharing and Disclosure

数据共享和披露

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

我们不会出售您的个人信息。我们可能与以下各方共享您的信息：

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist us in operating our platform
  • Project Partners: Other community members involved in the same projects
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • 服务提供商：帮助我们运营平台的受信任的第三方
  • 项目合作伙伴：参与同一项目的其他社区成员
  • 法律要求：当法律要求或需要保护我们的权利时
  • 业务转让：与合并、收购或资产出售相关时

Data Security

数据安全

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

我们实施适当的技术和组织措施，以保护您的个人信息免受未经授权的访问、更改、披露或破坏。

Your Rights

您的权利

You have the right to:

您有权：

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • 访问并接收您的个人数据副本

Community Guidelines

社区准则

Last Updated: October 2023

最后更新：2023年10月

Welcome to YNM TechConnect! To ensure a positive and productive environment for all members, please follow these community guidelines.

欢迎来到壹纳米技术交流社区！为确保为所有成员提供积极和富有成效的环境，请遵循以下社区准则。

Respect and Professionalism

尊重与专业性

  • Treat all community members with respect and professionalism
  • Use inclusive language and avoid discriminatory remarks
  • Respect different opinions and cultural backgrounds
  • Provide constructive feedback and avoid personal attacks
  • Maintain a positive and collaborative attitude
  • 以尊重和专业的态度对待所有社区成员
  • 使用包容性语言，避免歧视性言论
  • 尊重不同的意见和文化背景
  • 提供建设性反馈，避免人身攻击
  • 保持积极和协作的态度

Content and Communication

内容与沟通

  • Share relevant and valuable technical content
  • Respect intellectual property rights - only share content you have rights to
  • Avoid spamming, excessive self-promotion, or irrelevant content
  • Keep discussions focused on technology, projects, and professional development
  • Use appropriate channels for different types of communication
  • 分享相关且有价值的技术内容
  • 尊重知识产权 - 仅分享您有权分享的内容
  • 避免垃圾邮件、过度自我推广或不相关内容
  • 将讨论重点放在技术、项目和专业发展上
  • 为不同类型的沟通使用适当的渠道

Project Collaboration

项目协作

  • Be transparent about your skills, availability, and expectations
  • Meet project deadlines and communicate proactively about delays
  • Respect confidentiality agreements and project NDAs
  • Provide fair compensation for collaborative work
  • Resolve conflicts professionally and constructively
  • 在技能、可用性和期望方面保持透明
  • 满足项目截止日期，并主动沟通延迟情况
  • 尊重保密协议和项目保密条款
  • 为协作工作提供公平的报酬
  • 以专业和建设性的方式解决冲突

Prohibited Activities

禁止活动

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

以下活动严格禁止：

  • Harassment, bullying, or hate speech
  • Sharing malicious software or harmful content
  • Impersonation or false representation
  • Sharing others' personal information without consent
  • Any illegal activities or promotion of illegal services
  • Spamming or unauthorized advertising
  • Sharing explicit or inappropriate content
  • 骚扰、欺凌或仇恨言论
  • 分享恶意软件或有害内容
  • 冒充或虚假陈述
  • 未经同意分享他人的个人信息
  • 任何非法活动或推广非法服务
  • 垃圾邮件或未经授权的广告
  • 分享露骨或不适当的内容

Consequences of Violations

违规后果

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

违反这些准则可能导致：

  • Content removal
  • Temporary suspension of account
  • Permanent ban from the community
  • Legal action in severe cases
  • 内容删除
  • 账户暂时停用
  • 永久禁止进入社区
  • 在严重情况下采取法律行动

Reporting Issues

报告问题

If you encounter any violations of these guidelines or have concerns about community behavior, please report to:

如果您遇到任何违反这些准则的情况或对社区行为有疑虑，请向以下地址报告：
