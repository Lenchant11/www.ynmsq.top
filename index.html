Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

What are the requirements to join YNM TechConnect community? 加入壹纳米技术交流社区需要什么条件？
We welcome all tech talents interested in TikTok operations, Facebook growth, Google SEO, programming development, customer service technology and other fields. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced expert, as long as you are willing to share and learn, you can join our community.
我们欢迎所有对TikTok运营、Facebook增长、Google SEO、编程开发、客服技术等领域感兴趣的技术人才。无论您是初学者还是资深专家，只要愿意分享和学习，都可以加入我们的社区。
How does the community help members get project collaboration opportunities? 社区如何帮助成员获得项目协作机会？
We match suitable project opportunities with members with corresponding skills through the project release system within the community. Members can earn remuneration based on actual workload while accumulating project experience and industry connections.
我们通过社区内的项目发布系统，将合适的项目机会匹配给有相应技能的成员。成员可以按实际工作量获得报酬，同时积累项目经验和行业人脉。
What traffic growth support does the community provide? 社区提供哪些流量增长支持？
We provide community members with technical consultation for social platform operations, content strategy advice, and user growth method sharing. At the same time, experienced members in the community will also share their success stories and practical experience.
我们为社区成员提供社交平台运营的技术咨询、内容策略建议和用户增长方法分享。同时，社区内经验丰富的成员也会分享他们的成功案例和实践经验。
Is there a fee to join the community? 加入社区需要支付费用吗？
Basic community membership is free and allows participation in most technology exchanges and basic project collaborations. Some advanced features and services may have corresponding fees, but they will be clearly communicated and agreed upon by members.
基础社区成员是免费的，可以参与大部分技术交流和基础项目协作。部分高级功能和服务可能会有相应费用，但都会明确告知并征得成员同意。
